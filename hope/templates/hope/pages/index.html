About Us

There is hope foundation group, is the society created on 1st of august 2020, consisting of both undergraduates, graduates, other supporters outside the campus, Created with the purpose of bringing back the living hope to the thousands of people who are suffering outside over there, Read more..

Our Mission

To help them both physically, psychologically and spiritualy by providing them with their material needs, creating a friendship,socializing with them; But above all sharing with them the Words of life from the HOLY BIBLE.

Our Plan

To have one charity event after every two months, where we will visit one of the groups in need,i.e orphans in their orphanage center and the line will follow up, producing Tshirt with the Brand There is hope,..

Our Vision

Our vision is to restore and revive back the living Hope to the thousands of people who are passing through hard times in this life and those who are at the edge of giving up their lives, and showing them the Light of truth...
