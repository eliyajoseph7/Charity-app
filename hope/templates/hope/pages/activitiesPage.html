Our Activities

1. VISITING THE ORPHAGE CENTERS

The intention of visiting these centers is to relieve sufferings and necessities of orphans by supplying their needs but also to socialize with them in order to provide psychological support. Providing a session of encouragement from the Word of God, accompanied with prayers will help in restoring back the hope to their lives

2. COMFORTING PATIENTS FROM DIFFERENT HOSPITALS

Nevertheless, that we are providing physical needs to those who are sick, but a tender visit and comfort in their chronic illness accompanied with promises from the Word of God and sweet words of prayers and love, will avail much and cause the cords of hope that were broken to vibrate once more and rays of the living hope will keep on shining in their Hearts.

3. TO VISIT PRISONERS

Another step that is crucial is to comfort the prisoners, helping them with their basic needs, distributing to them books that will help them to find the real purpose of life and revive back their living hope.

4. SUPPORTING ELDERLY PEOPLE

Many elderly individuals suffer from loneliness, neglect and psychological distress due to their dependence, as the team we have to take serious measures in supporting and influencing the lives of elderly people, both physical, psychological and spiritual support are needed by this special group, with all ability on our hands we are striving to bring the smile upon the faces of this beloveds.

5. VISITING SCHOOLS AND IMPACTING YOUTHS LIFE

Through motivations, and providing good presentations that will enlights the mind of many youth will bring a great impact upon many students. It will be the seed that will germinate and produce many fruits. Prevention is better than cure.
