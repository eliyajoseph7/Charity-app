About Us

There is hope foundation group, is the society created on 1st of august 2020, consisting of both undergraduates, graduates, other supporters outside the campus, Created with the purpose of bringing back the living hope to the thousands of people who are suffering outside over there, this includes, visiting orphanage centers, hospitals, drug addicted centers, and prisoners.

The group is open to any individual who will be touched to support this foundation in either way ,i.e providing money, clothes , Bibles , and other materials which will be helpfull while we will be conducting the Charity events;may God bless you as plan to support His dearly Children;

Our Mission

The Greatest mission complies with the mission of Christ in luke 19;10 For the son of man has come to seek and to save that which was lost knowing this There is Hope foundation group have joined hands together to support differrent people and beloved who are passing through hard times , this includes , orphans , prisoners, patients who are in the hospitals,and the Drug addicted people.

The organisation will help them both physically, psychologically and spiritualy by providing them with their material needs, creating a friendship,socializing with them; But above all sharing with them the Words of life from the HOLY BIBLE.

Our Plan

There is hope foundation group has got different amazing plans to fullfill their dreams as follows;

  1. To have one charity event after every two months, where we will visit one of the groups in need,i.e orphans in their orphanage center and the line will follow up.
  2. Every member who will be willing to join the team will contribute 5000/=Tsh ( 2 USD) as the subscription fee per month to sustain all plans and execution of the charity event Planed
  3. The organisation has created a project plan of producing Tshirt with the Brand There is hope, and all of the profit shall be directed to support the Foundation in all of its activities

Our Vision

Our vision is to restore and revive back the living Hope to the thousands of people who are passing through hard times in this life and those who are at the edge of giving up their lives, and showing them the Light of truth and hope in this degenerate age.
